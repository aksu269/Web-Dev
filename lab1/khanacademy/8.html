Interesting facts about Miami

1. It was founded by a woman

Miami is the only major U.S. city founded by a woman, Julia Tuttle, who convinced developers to extend the railroad there.

2. "Magic city"

The nickname came from its incredibly fast growth from a remote swamp to a bustling city.

3. Built on swamp

The area was originally swampland, significantly altered for development.
