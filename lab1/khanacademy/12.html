Recipe #1

  • Time: 10
  • Serves: 1
Ingredients Quantity
Tomato 1
Onion 2
Pepper 1
Lime 1
Sesame oil 1 ts
Peanut butter 3 ts

What you'll need:

  • Sharp knife
  • Cutting board
  • Large bowl
  • Serving spoon

Step 1: Cut up all veggies (tomato, onion, pepper, lime).

Step 2: Combine all veggies into a bowl.

Step 3: Add lime juice or squeeze fresh lime.

Step 4: Mix in seasonings and peanut butter.

Step 5: Drizzle with sesame oil.

Recipe #2

  • Time: 10
  • Serves: 1
Ingredients Quantity
Dried shiitake mushrooms 1
Cabbage 2
Firm tofu 1
Potato 1
Sesame oil 1 ts
Green onion 3 ts
Garlic and ginger 3 ts
Soy sauce 3 ts

What you'll need:

  • Sharp knife
  • Cutting board
  • Large bowl
  • Round dumpling wrapper (gow gee pastry)

Step 1: Wilt cabbage and rehydrate shiitake mushrooms. Mix with remaining filling ingredients.

Step 2:Wrap dumplings, pan-fry 2 minutes, then steam 5 minutes (same pan).

Recipe #3

  • Time: 10
  • Serves: 1
Ingredients Quantity
Lean beef chuck 1-1.5
Water 3L
Flour 3 cups
Salt 2 ts
Potato 2
Green onion 1
Carrot 1
Butter 1 ts

What you'll need:

  • Sharp knife
  • Cutting board
  • Large bowl
  • Pan

Step 1: Wash the meat and put it in a saucepan. Add bay leaf and peppercorns. Pour 2 liters of cold water, put on fire and bring to a boil. Remove the foam. After 10 minutes, remove the laurel. Cook for 2-2.5 hours.

Step 2:Mix the sifted flour, egg, salt and half a cup of water. Knead the dough.

Step 3:Divide the dough into 5 pieces and roll each into a very thin cake. Put the tortillas on paper and leave for 30-40 minutes to dry.

Step 4:Peel potatoes and onions. Cut the potatoes into large pieces, and the onion into half rings.

Step 5:In 30 minutes. Add salt to the broth and potatoes to the pan before cooking.

Step 6:Remove the cooked meat and potatoes from the soup. Cut the meat into medium-sized pieces and return to the broth, heat for 1-2 minutes. Put the potatoes in a saucepan.

Step 7:Add the chopped onion to the potatoes, sprinkle thickly with ground pepper, and pour in 2 ladles of broth. Cook for 5 minutes. Cut the dried tortillas into 4-5 pieces and put them in a saucepan with boiling broth.

Step 8:Cook for 4-5 minutes, stirring occasionally. Put the cooked tortillas, potatoes and onions on plates, pour in the broth and add the pieces of meat. You can decorate with fresh herbs.
